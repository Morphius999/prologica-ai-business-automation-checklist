# Pro Logica AI Business Automation Checklist

A clean, lightweight static checklist tool that helps business owners identify where AI automation can improve business workflows, customer communication, internal operations, reporting, document handling, and decision support.

The checklist helps review repetitive workflows, customer support, lead intake, document processing, email and communication, reporting, knowledge management, CRM and business systems, task routing, security, integrations, and AI governance.

Live Demo: https://morphius999.github.io/prologica-ai-business-automation-checklist/

This free checklist was created by Pro Logica to help business owners identify where AI automation may improve real business workflows.

Built by Pro Logica: https://www.prologica.ai

## Why This Exists

AI automation works best when it is tied to real workflows, clear data sources, human review points, and measurable operational outcomes. This checklist gives owners and operators a practical starting point before a discovery call or AI automation project.

## Features

- Twelve AI business automation planning categories
- Progress percentage that updates as checklist items are completed
- AI automation readiness level based on completion percentage
- Browser-saved progress with localStorage
- Reset button to clear saved checklist progress
- Mobile responsive static HTML, CSS, and JavaScript

## Setup

Open `index.html` in a browser.

No frameworks, npm packages, or external dependencies are required.
